# Coach Leaderboard & Performance System (Future Phase)

## Purpose

Create a system that rewards high-performing coaches and physicians on the My Perfect Meals platform while encouraging client results, engagement, and platform growth.

This system is designed to:

- motivate coaches to grow the platform
- reward client success, not just sales
- highlight trusted professionals
- automatically monitor performance using platform data

This system will be implemented **after Android and Master Shopping List systems are complete**.

---

## Core Concept

The platform will maintain a **monthly coach leaderboard** based on three categories:

1. **Client Outcomes**
2. **Client Engagement**
3. **Platform Growth**

The leaderboard will be calculated automatically by the platform.

---

## Leaderboard Metrics

### Client Outcomes

Measures improvement in client compliance and progress.

Possible signals:

- meal logging consistency
- macro compliance score improvement
- weekly adherence rate
- weight trend improvements

This prevents coaches from gaming the system with inactive clients.

---

### Client Engagement

Measures how actively a coach manages their clients.

Signals:

- tablet message response time
- number of active clients
- client retention duration
- coaching activity

---

### Platform Growth

Measures contribution to platform growth.

Signals:

- referral code usage
- new client signups through coach code
- community contribution

---

## Coach Reward Tiers

Base commission is **25%**.

Tier upgrades increase earnings.

### Bronze Coach

25% commission
Default tier

### Silver Coach

27.5% commission

Requirements:
- positive ratings
- active clients
- platform participation

### Gold Coach

30% commission

Requirements:
- high client satisfaction
- consistent retention
- strong engagement

## Coach Badges

High performing coaches receive badges.

Examples:

```
Gold Coach
Top 10% Performance
```

Badges appear on:

- coach profiles
- coach discovery page
- client selection screens

---

## Moderation Integration

The leaderboard system integrates with the **coach rating and strike system**.

Signals that may lower ranking:

- repeated complaints
- abusive communication
- poor client outcomes

The platform may issue **strikes** automatically.

Three strikes may result in:

- leaderboard removal
- ProCare suspension
- permanent removal (in severe cases)

---

## Future AI Component

An AI system will calculate performance scores using:

- compliance data

---

## Implementation Phase

This feature will be built **after**:

1. Android version
2. Master Shopping List / grocery system
3. Delivery integration

---

## Notes

The leaderboard must never reward **sales only**.
Client outcomes and engagement must carry the most weight.

This protects the reputation of the platform and prevents unethical coaching behavior.

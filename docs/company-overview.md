# My Perfect Meals — Company Overview
**Version:** May 2026 | **Classification:** Internal / Enterprise-Facing

---

## 1. Company Overview

My Perfect Meals is an AI-powered nutrition and meal-planning platform built for consumers, fitness professionals, and clinical care teams. The platform delivers personalized meal generation, dietary tracking, biometric monitoring, and professional coaching infrastructure through a single, integrated ecosystem available on web and iOS.

The platform is architected around a core belief: that personalized nutrition should be accessible, practical, and safe for every type of person — from someone trying to eat better to a physician managing a patient with oncology-related dietary restrictions.

**Core problems the platform solves:**

- Consumers have no practical way to get personalized, safe, and food-culture-aware meal guidance without hiring a nutritionist or dietitian.
- Fitness coaches and trainers have tools for tracking workouts but no integrated system for delivering and managing client nutrition programs.
- Nutrition certification graduates learn science but are not prepared with the practical tooling required to run a professional meal-planning practice from day one.
- Clinical practitioners lack software that enforces dietary safety rules (allergens, medical conditions, drug-nutrient interactions) at the AI generation level, not just as a checkbox disclaimer.
- Families have no unified platform that handles multiple household members with different dietary identities, restrictions, and goals under a single plan.

**The ecosystem includes:**
- An AI meal generation engine spanning 12+ distinct creation tools
- A professional coaching and client management layer (ProCare)
- Clinical protocol integrations for GLP-1, diabetes, oncology, anti-inflammatory, thyroid, kidney disease, and heart failure populations
- A household and family plan infrastructure supporting up to 4 profiles
- A real-time biometrics and lab metrics tracking system
- A grocery and shopping intelligence engine with retail-unit consolidation
- A restaurant, fast food, and dining guidance system

| Chef's Kitchen | Guided AI cooking experience |
| Holiday Feast | Extended multi-course holiday meal planning |
| Potluck Planner | Group meal coordination and planning |

All generators share a single Protocol Envelope enforcement model. No generator produces output without assembling and enforcing the user's complete constraint profile first.

### Weekly Meal Board
A structured 7-day meal planning board where meals can be placed, replaced, and organized by day and meal slot. Supports coach-assigned locked days, custom meal templates, and meal plan archiving.

### Macro System
- Daily macro targets (calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat) derived from user biometrics and goals
- Manual macro override capability
- Macro logging and adherence tracking
- Debug-level macro audit logging for development and clinical verification
- Macro Truth Contract: AI is prohibited from inventing macro values. Null means unknown; 0 means confirmed zero. Values are rejected or regenerated, never mutated.

### Grocery and Shopping Intelligence
- Master shopping list aggregated from generated meals across the meal board
- Consolidation engine: deduplicates items by normalized name, sums quantities, converts to human-readable retail units (1 bunch, 3.5 lb, 1 bag)
- Retail Intelligence layer: produce weight table (60+ entries), meat/seafood weight conversion, grain/pantry container logic, fresh herb display, leafy green display, citrus display
- Direct integration with major grocery delivery retailers (Instacart, Walmart, and others) on tablet and desktop
- PDF and text export of consolidated shopping lists

### Smart Scan / Ingredient Intelligence
- Camera-based food and ingredient scanning
- Personalized label analysis filtered through the user's dietary identity and medical constraints
- Flags allergens, restricted ingredients, and protocol violations specific to the scanning user

### Restaurant and Dining Guidance

## 7. Competitive Differentiators

**Protocol-first AI enforcement.**
The constraint hierarchy is not a filter applied after generation. It is assembled before any AI call is made and enforced at the prompt construction level. Dietary identity, allergies, medical hard limits, medical optimization, avoidances, and preferences are applied in strict layered order. No other consumer nutrition platform operates with this architecture.

**Clinical-grade protocols in a consumer product.**
Oncology, kidney disease, heart failure, and GLP-1 protocols exist as dedicated, physician-assignable systems — not as generic "low-sodium" flags. These are built with the same structural rigor as clinical decision support software, delivered through a consumer-grade interface.

**Complete professional infrastructure.**
The platform does not just generate meals — it delivers a full coach-client operating system. ProCare includes client onboarding, program delivery, communication, biometrics monitoring, board control, and multi-coach studio management. Coaches do not need a second tool.

**Macro Truth Contract.**
The platform enforces a documented standard for macro data integrity: AI cannot invent macro values. Values are verified or absent. This matters for clinical users, coaches managing client compliance, and any integration with health record systems.

**12+ distinct generation tools.**
Most meal planning platforms offer one generation model. My Perfect Meals offers over twelve, each calibrated for a specific need — from a Tuesday night craving to a multi-course Thanksgiving feast to a GLP-1 user's post-injection snack.

**Food culture and cuisine intelligence.**
Generation is informed by cultural and cuisine preferences that are treated as first-class constraints, not suggestions. A Halal user does not receive a meal with a Halal option substituted in. The dietary identity container is the outer wall of every generation call.

**Household architecture.**
The platform is built to serve families, not just individuals. Multiple profiles, independent protocols, shared subscriptions, and profile-switching are native to the system — not bolt-ons.

## 9. Enterprise and White-Label Direction

**Multi-tenant infrastructure:**
The platform is architected with studio-based multi-tenancy (coach studios, client rosters, workspace isolation) as a foundation. The path to full white-label deployment is an extension of the existing studio and creator system layers, not a rebuild.

**Coach and provider ecosystem:**
The ProCare platform is designed to scale from a solo practitioner with 5 clients to a multi-coach organization with 150 or more. The slug-based coach registry allows new coaches to be onboarded through configuration changes with no code deployment required.

**Educational institution potential:**
The platform is a direct fit for fitness and nutrition certification curriculum bundles. Key capabilities:
- Dual-role subscriptions (a single user can operate as both coach and client simultaneously)
- Classroom simulation: one student acts as coach, one as client; both experience the full workflow
- Product code provisioning: institutions can distribute activation codes at enrollment, ensuring every graduate enters their career already proficient with the tool
- No licensing complexity for the institution: students activate their own accounts through a code
- Graduates become independent subscribers post-certification, creating an organic acquisition pipeline from institutional relationships

**Healthcare integrations:**
The clinical protocol layer is already built to physician-assignment standards. Near-term integrations with EHR systems, telehealth platforms, and nutrition-focused clinical workflows are a natural extension of the existing care team and physician portal infrastructure.

## 10. Technical Foundation Summary

My Perfect Meals is built on a modern, fully typed full-stack architecture designed for reliability, safety, and rapid iteration.

**Application layer:**
The client is a React 18 single-page application using TypeScript throughout. The interface is built with Tailwind CSS and Radix UI component primitives, optimized for mobile-first use while scaling cleanly to tablet and desktop. The iOS application uses Capacitor for native shell and in-app purchase handling.

**Server layer:**
The backend is an Express.js API server running on Node.js 20, also fully written in TypeScript. All routes are registered through a single audited route registry with middleware-enforced authentication on every protected endpoint.

**Database:**
PostgreSQL is the primary data store, accessed via Drizzle ORM with a fully typed schema. The schema is divided between shared types (available to both client and server) and server-only tables for sensitive clinical and coaching data.

**AI system:**
All meal generation and guidance is powered by the OpenAI API. The platform's Protocol Envelope service assembles a complete user constraint profile before any AI call — dietary identity, allergens, medical hard limits, medical optimization layers, avoidances, and preferences — and enforces it at prompt construction. A post-generation scan validates every AI output before it is returned to the user.

**Safety architecture:**
The Macro Truth Contract prevents the AI from inventing nutritional values. Clinical prompt builders for each supported medical condition enforce condition-specific ingredient restrictions and macro targets. Hard-blocked ingredients are enforced at both the prompt layer and via post-generation content scanning.

**Integrations:**
- Stripe: subscription billing, checkout, and webhook processing
- Apple: in-app purchase receipt verification (iOS)
- Resend: transactional email
- Twilio: SMS delivery and inbound messaging
- VAPID: web push notifications
- Object storage: user media uploads with authenticated signing

**Operational infrastructure:**
The platform runs in a continuously deployed environment with a validated pre-push hook system, health check endpoints, and an admin dashboard for moderation and content review. A Creator System layer allows brand customization of AI output styling without modifying core safety or dietary constraint enforcement.

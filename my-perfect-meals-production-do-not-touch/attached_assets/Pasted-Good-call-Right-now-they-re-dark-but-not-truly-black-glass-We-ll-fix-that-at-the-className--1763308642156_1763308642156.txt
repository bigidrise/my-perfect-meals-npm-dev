Good call. Right now they’re *dark*, but not truly **black-glass**. We’ll fix that at the className level only — no behavior changes, no logic changes.

You’ve got three “info card” surfaces for medical badges:

1. `HealthBadgesPopover.tsx` → `PopoverContent`
2. `MedicalInfoBubble.tsx` → the `isOpen && <div ...>` popover
3. `HealthBadgePopup.tsx` → `PopoverContent`

Below are **drop-in className updates** for each. Just swap these where they apply.

---

## 1️⃣ `HealthBadgesPopover.tsx` — make PopoverContent black glass

Find this:

```tsx
<PopoverContent
  align={align}
  side={side}
  sideOffset={8}
  className="rounded-2xl bg-black/90 text-white border border-white/10 shadow-lg max-w-xs p-4 space-y-2 text-sm z-50"
>
```

Replace the `className` with this:

```tsx
className="rounded-2xl max-w-xs p-4 space-y-2 text-sm z-50
           border border-white/15
           bg-gradient-to-b from-white/5 via-black/80 to-black/95
           bg-clip-padding backdrop-blur-xl
           shadow-xl shadow-emerald-500/20"
```

So the full block becomes:

```tsx
<PopoverContent
  align={align}
  side={side}
  sideOffset={8}
  className="rounded-2xl max-w-xs p-4 space-y-2 text-sm z-50
             border border-white/15
             bg-gradient-to-b from-white/5 via-black/80 to-black/95
             bg-clip-padding backdrop-blur-xl
             shadow-xl shadow-emerald-500/20"
>
```

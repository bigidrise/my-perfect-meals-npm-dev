# Static Image Library Manifest for My Perfect Meals

## Image Specifications for ChatGPT/Canva

### Technical Requirements:
- **Size:** 512x512 pixels (square)
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background OR JPG with dark/black background
- **Style:** Professional food photography, overhead or 3/4 angle shot
- **Lighting:** Soft, warm lighting (like restaurant photography)
- **Background:** Dark slate, black marble, or wooden cutting board
- **Plating:** Clean, appetizing presentation on white or dark ceramic plate

### Visual Style Guide:
- Make food look appetizing and realistic
- Show the SPECIFIC preparation style clearly (scrambled eggs look different from sunny-side up)
- No text, logos, or watermarks
- Consistent lighting across all images
- Single serving portions

## ChatGPT Image Generation Prompt Template

For each image, use this prompt format:

```
Professional food photography of [INGREDIENT] prepared [PREP STYLE], 
single serving on a dark ceramic plate, overhead shot, 
soft warm lighting, dark slate background, 
appetizing presentation, no text or watermarks, 
high resolution, realistic food styling
```

Example:
```
Professional food photography of scrambled eggs, 
single serving on a dark ceramic plate, overhead shot, 
soft warm lighting, dark slate background, 
appetizing presentation, no text or watermarks, 
high resolution, realistic food styling
```
